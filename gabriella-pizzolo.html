Biography

Gabriella Pizzolo was born in Schenectady, NY on March 10, 2003. Musical theatre prodigy who rocketed to fame first appearing on Broadway in the acclaimed musical Matilda. She then went on to appear on Broadway’s Fun Home. In 2019, she joined season three of Netflix’s Stranger Things as Suzie. She understudied for Tony nominee Sydney Lucas in Fun Home before taking on the role of Small Alison herself. Before earning her Broadway debut, she appeared in several regional productions of plays like Les Miserables, Ragtime, and Miracle on 34th Street starting at the age of seven.
