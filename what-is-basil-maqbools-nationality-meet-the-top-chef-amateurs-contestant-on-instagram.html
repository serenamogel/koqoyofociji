What Is Basil Maqbools Nationality? Meet The Top Chef Amateurs Contestant On Instagram

August 2024 · 2 minute read

Top Chef Amateurs’ contestant Basil Maqbool’s nationality seems to be Pakistani-American. To learn more about him, keep reading the article.

Basil Maqbool is a pharmacist who is currently the CEO of California Independent Pharmacies, Inc., which is a parent company to Vail Ranch Pharmacy in Temecula, California.

He is best known for being the contestant in the reality TV show Top Chef Amateurs at Bravo TV. 

Basil obtained a Biomedical Sciences degree in 1998 from the University of California, Riverside. He then went on to earn the degree of Pharm-D from the Midwestern University College of Pharmacy, Glendale, in 2001.

Beginning his career at the largest independent pharmacy in San Diego, he decided to go on to business for himself. And in 2002, he purchased Medicine World pharmacy in Hemet, California but later sold in 2005. Since then, he worked for a few years as a relief pharmacist and opened Vail Ranch Pharmacy in 2008.
