Who Is Shawn Booth Baby Mama?

Shawn Booth, a former contestant on The Bachelorette, shared exciting news about expecting a baby with his girlfriend on his podcast, In the Booth with Shawn Booth.

He hasn’t revealed who the baby’s mother is, as he prefers to keep his private life private.

Since his breakup with Kaitlyn Bristowe in 2018, Shawn has been very secretive about his personal life. However, with the news of becoming a father, he’s now opening up more to his fans.

On his podcast episode on July 17, he revealed that the baby is due on December 12 and expressed his excitement about becoming a father. He mentioned that the mother is someone he’s known and had a relationship with for a long time.

Although he didn’t share the mother’s identity, Shawn hopes she’ll join him on the podcast soon. He also disclosed that she is 18 weeks pregnant, and they’ve decided not to determine the baby’s gender before birth.

Shawn described how overwhelmed and incredible the experience is, but he also acknowledged that being a dad differs from the mom’s perspective. He’s eager to support and help out as much as he can during this journey into parenthood.
