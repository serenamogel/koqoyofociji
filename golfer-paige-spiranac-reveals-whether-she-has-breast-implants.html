Golfer Paige Spiranac reveals whether she has breast implants

August 2024 · 2 minute read

Paige Spiranac wants the world to know she is genetically blessed.

The pro golfer-turned-industry sex symbol took to her Instagram Story Monday to answer a variety of fan questions, revealing that her breasts are, in fact, “real.”

One inquiring follower then asked the 30-year-old golf instructor whether her bust was “always that big.”

Spiranac said many people have pointed out that her “body looks different” and breasts “look smaller” in throwback pictures, which she acknowledged as true.

“I weighed significantly less [back then], and since they are real, they change depending on weight loss and weight gain,” she explained.

“And so since I have gained more weight, they have gotten bigger,” she added, noting that “being on birth control” has also contributed.

“There are a bunch of different changes — hormonal, weight gain — that make them change size,” Spiranac continued, choosing to divulge even more personal information.

According to the blond bombshell, her bosom grows “during [her menstrual] cycle.”

But she doesn’t seem to mind that her chest gets so much attention, given that she admittedly “like[s] to show them off.”

The social media influencer and podcast host went on to shut down another rumor pertaining to her boobs, telling her fans that her nipples are not pierced.

“I have zero tattoos and zero piercings,” she said, noting that “there was a theory going around because of a shirt I wore [with] a weird gapping that they were pierced, [but] they are not pierced. Sounds very painful.”

Spiranac shared that she used to have her earlobes pierced when she was younger, “but they closed up because [she] would never wear [her] earrings.”

She’s simply “not a jewelry person.”

As for tattoos, the Colorado native “love[s] them on other people” but doesn’t foresee herself getting any because she doesn’t believe she could “ever pull [them] off.”
